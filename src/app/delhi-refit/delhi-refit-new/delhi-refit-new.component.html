Ser. Description Remarks
(i) Check pipe system is clean and clear from obstruction.
(ii) Rotate the pumps, by hand, in direction indicated on the driving end cover.
(iii) Check that all valves are in the open position.
(iv) Start the pump and check correct rotation.
(v) Check suction and discharge pressure of the pump.
