1. Fuel Transfer Pump

(a). Carry out following checks before starting the pump.
Ser Description Sat/Unsat
(i) Check pipe system is clean and clear from obstruction
(ii) Prime the pumps with fuel oil drawn from the supply system to ensure that the pump will not run in a dry unlubricated condition.
(iii) Rotate the pumps, by hand, in direction indicated on the driving end cover.
(iv) Check that the functional position of all valves should correspond with the valve position indicators.
(v) Check that all valves are in the open position.
(vii) Ensure all deck fuel receiving valve should be closed
(viii) Switch ON starter and check rotation of motor
(ix) Check pump for sign of overheating over a 10minute period
(x) Open suction from one of the Aft tanks No. 10 and 22
(xi) Repeat the above fuel transfer procedure for aft fuel tanks no. 10 to 22 and check time taken from start to pump working
